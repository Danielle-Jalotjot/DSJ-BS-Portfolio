About Me


At 22 years old, my art has taken new forms and is expressed through both traditional and digital mediums. Currently enrolled at San Jose State University in the Digital Media Art BFA Program, I look forward to creative experimentation in a more technical sense. I aspire to work in 3D animation, where I can contribute to the same stories and characters I adored as a child.



Traditional and Digital Work

My journey in the digital art world began a year ago, as I've been using mediums like graphite, ink, and acrylic paint for most of my art. With new traditional projects balancing with my personal work, I find myself exploring and experimenting in both worlds of expression.
